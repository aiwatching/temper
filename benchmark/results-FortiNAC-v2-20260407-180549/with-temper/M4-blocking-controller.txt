Done. Changes made:

**Controller** — removed `Mono.deferContextual`, `subscribeOn`, `onErrorResume`, `doFinally`, and the `nettyOffloadExecutor` dependency. The method now:
1. Prepares the request
2. Registers the callback future
3. Publishes to Redis

5. Returns `ResultDTO<?>` directly, with try-catch for timeout/error handling
